Concept 1
Split View
Map and trip list always visible side by side — no toggling needed. Warm amber sidebar for your travel log, cool dark map on the left. Click a trip and the map zooms + highlights the route. The world-map view slides in as a panel over the sidebar.

Concept 2
Globe Hero
The world map takes the full top half — your trips light up as dots on the globe. Below it: horizontal postcard cards filtered by continent. No separate "world view" needed. Clicking a card zooms the globe and shows photos. Feels like Google Earth meets a travel feed.
Globe as hero Postcard scroll Continent filter No view tabs Navy + amber

Concept 3
Film Strip
Map takes the full screen. At the bottom: a horizontal film strip of all trips — scrub through them like a roll of film. The selected trip shows an overlay card on the map with its photo, dates, and description. Dark charcoal base with warm gold for the active trip. Very cinematic.

Concept 4
Expedition Board
Feels like a military ops map + field journal. Map left, compact travel log right. Active pin shows coordinates. Deep forest green/teal on near-black — like night-vision. The world map is integrated as a toggle within the same layout. Precision and adventure.
